Anne Frank Memorial Project

Xcode

The Anne Frank Memorial Project is an Augmented Reality experience designed for the Anne Frank Memorial in Boise. It covers the history of discrimination and its connections to modern day hate-based ideologies, and is designed to fight against radicalization. I was one of the primary programmers on this project, and I was learning to use Swift and Xcode while working on this project, as it was my first time using the language and platform.

ACHD Roundabout Project

Unity

This project is a Virtual Reality simulation of a roundabout, designed for teachingpeople how to properly use a roundabout in an immersive, low-stress environment. I was one of the programmers on the project's team, focused primarily on building the in-car experience in the latter end of the game.
